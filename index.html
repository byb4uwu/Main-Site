Week 15 Recap: Semifinals

12/17/2019

Gurley Gone Crazy (144.96) dominated Wilson1getmeabeer (100.84) in the first semifinal matchup of week 15. Wilson's biggest stars Amari Cooper (2.4) and Melvin Gordon (4.9) didn't show up at the critical time. Nevertheless, it was a great overall season, and a chance to finish third still remains. Gurley had double-digit point performances from 7 diffferent players, including Davante Adams (19.8) and Todd Gurley (19.3). Gurley Gone Crazy has improbably reached the championship game eight weeks from being 2-5 and in 11th place. They've done it with a lineup of Ryan Tannehill (25.16, unowned until December), Tyler Higbee (17.1, unowned until last week), and A.J. Brown (21.4, dropped by three other teams this season).

Baby got Mack (127.52) easily disposed of Team Double Dipp (106.6) to sail into the title matchup. Double Dipp's Patrick Mahomes (23.7) and Michael Thomas (24.8) couldn't save the rest of the group from ultimate demise. Mack rode his horse Ezequiel Elliott (29.5) to victory as he often does. His flattening of the rest of the league all started with a phenomenal draft. Picks Deshaun Watson (18.92), Kenny Golladay (5.9), Austin Ekeler (10.6), Dallas Goedert (8), and Elliott have made the ride to the top seem like a breeze. Let's see if Baby got Mack can seal the deal in week 16.
