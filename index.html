Summary of League Settings Changes and Notes

New Position: This one was pretty split so we're going to add a regular Flex (R/W/T) position to the lineup. The idea behind adding another position is to deepen the usable player pool and increase competition.
Kickers: While I am strongly against this, we're going to keep kickers in the lineup this year due overall support for the option. I'll keep trying to convince everyone I'm right and you're wrong.
Waivers: $100 FAAB budget will be implemented, with waivers processing Tuesday night. Please read up on the rules of FAAB or please ask questions if you need clarification on how it works.
Playoffs: Top 5 head-to-head records plus the next best total points will make the playoffs, with the top 2 records receiving first round byes.
Defense: Defensive scoring will be tweaked slightly. I think this is a more balanced scoring system.
Prize: $15 buy-in. 1st Place - $120, 2nd Place - $45, 3rd Place - $15. Please Venmo me (@Gautam-Rao-1) before the draft. If you don't have Venmo, join the 21st century or let me know and we can figure something out.
Draft: August 25, 2019 @ 4pm EDT. Mark you calendars. This seems to be the best time to draft to accommodate everyone's needs. Let me know if you can't make this date.
Draft Order for next year: This will be determined by the winner of the consolation bracket (6 non-playoff qualifying teams). Details TBD.
