Week 14 Recap: Quarterfinals

12/10/2019

In quarterfinal matchup 1, defending champion Fuck you Mason (124.6) was ousted by Team Double Dipp (125.22). This one surely stings for Fuck you Mason, as Rashaad Penny (2.1) was fractional points away from closing the book on Double Dipp's Cinderella-type run before suffering an ACL injury. Leaving Darius Slayton (29.5) and Jared Cook (19.4) on the bench just pours salt on the wound. Double Dipp's magical ride as a 6-7 regular season team will continue and may hinge on the health of Patrick Mahomes (14.92) and a ragtag bunch of RBs including Patrick Laird (10.6) and Adrian Peterson (13.6). A week 15 date with king-of-the-hill Baby got Mack awaits.

Gurley Gone Crazy (108.54) bounced Get Ridley Of 'Em (99.26) in the other quarterfinal pairing. Ridley's attempt to exploit the Eli Manning-led Giants with Philadelphia (4) backfired and Miles Sanders (8.9) lost touches to Boston Scott when the rookie left the game due to cramping. Benny Snell (2.1) showed very little talent on the field other than falling over at first contact in his start for the injured James Conner. Gurley made a QB swap this week and benefited from Ryan Tannehill's (28.54) demolition of the Raiders' secondary. Tannehill has a fallback as a defensive player if the whole quarterbacking thing doesn't work out. Davante Adams (6.1) will need to step it up if Gurley expects to advance to the finals in week 16.
